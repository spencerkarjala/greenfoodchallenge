## Green Food Challenge

The Green Food Challenge is an app that aims to help people reduce greenhouse gas emissions in Metro Vancouver through making small yet powerful changes to their diet. Greenhouse gases are measured in CO2e and are known to directly contribute to climate change. Since food production is a significant source of CO2e, we can simply choose to consume foods that take less CO2e to produce than othersâ€¦ and our app hopes to help you with exactly that!
